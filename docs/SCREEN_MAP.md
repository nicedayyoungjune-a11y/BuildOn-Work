## MVP Flow Summary

1. Worker signs up.
2. Company registers a site.
3. Company posts a job.
4. Worker views and applies to the job.
5. Company reviews applicants and confirms attendance.
6. Worker checks confirmed work status.
7. Site manager manages attendance status.
8. Users check payment option and expected settlement information.
