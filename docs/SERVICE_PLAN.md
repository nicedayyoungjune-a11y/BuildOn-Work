# Service Plan

## Overview

BuildOn Work is a webapp-based construction workforce platform that connects construction daily workers with construction companies and site managers.

The product aims to become a trusted platform in the construction labor market, similar to the position miso.kr holds in home services.

## Product Direction

BuildOn Work combines:

- GADA's construction job matching strength
- miso.kr's easy and approachable user experience

## Initial Market

Initial regions:

- Gyeonggi-do
- Chungcheong-do

Primary users:

1. Construction daily workers
2. Skilled workers and skilled worker teams
3. Construction companies and site managers

## Core Value

For workers:

- Find nearby construction jobs more easily.
- Check work confirmation status clearly.
- Understand payment option and expected settlement information.

For companies and site managers:

- Register sites and job posts simply.
- Review applicants quickly.
- Confirm attendance and manage work status in one flow.

## Competitive Position

BuildOn Work should not copy GADA or miso.kr directly.

The service should focus on construction-specific job matching while keeping the user experience simple enough for daily workers and site managers to use without friction.
